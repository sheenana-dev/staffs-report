# B-Ticket Department Overview

## Company Context

B-Ticket is a digital coupon marketplace (B2B2C) operating in the Philippines. SME merchants list promotional deals, and consumers claim them via mobile app and website. Revenue comes from user subscriptions and promotional banners inside the app.

## Departments

### IT Department (5-8 people)
- **Owns:** Mobile apps (iOS, Android, Huawei), web platform, backend APIs, infrastructure, UI/UX
- **Key output:** Working product, stable platform, new features, user experience design
- **Reports via:** Staff GC (daily) + Management GC (lead reports) + sprint board
- **Cadence:** Daily standups, 2-week sprints, monthly retrospectives

### Design Department (3 people)
- **Owns:** Graphic design, merchant marketing materials, brand identity, campaign visuals, app store visuals
- **Key output:** Design deliverables, merchant collateral, promotional assets
- **Reports via:** Staff GC (daily) + Management GC (lead reports)
- **Cadence:** Daily check-ins, weekly design reviews

### Sales Department (2 people + Onboarding Ops + Account Management Ops)
- **Owns:** Merchant acquisition, promotional banner sales
- **Sub-roles:**
  - **Sales (2 people):** Find and close new merchants
  - **Onboarding Ops:** Collect merchant details (photos, operating hours, contract), input into HubSpot and B-Ticket app
  - **Account Management Ops:** Monitor coupon redemptions monthly, work with merchants to swap underperforming coupons
- **Key output:** New merchant sign-ups, banner revenue, merchant retention
- **Reports via:** Staff GC (daily) + Management GC (lead reports) + HubSpot
- **Cadence:** Daily activity updates, weekly pipeline review, monthly account reviews

### Marketing Department (5 people)
- **Owns:** User acquisition, brand awareness, content, social media, campaigns
- **Key output:** App downloads, user engagement, campaign results
- **Reports via:** Staff GC (daily) + Management GC (lead reports)
- **Cadence:** Daily content updates, weekly campaign check-ins, monthly analytics review

## Cross-Department Dependencies

```
Sales ←→ Marketing    Sales brings merchants, Marketing drives users to redeem their coupons
Sales ←→ Design       Design creates merchant onboarding materials and promo visuals
IT ←→ Design          Design provides specs, IT implements them in the app
IT ←→ Marketing       Marketing needs landing pages, analytics tracking, push notifications
IT ←→ Sales           Sales needs CRM integrations, merchant dashboard features
Design ←→ Marketing   Design produces campaign visuals and social media assets
```

**How it works:**
- **Staff GC:** All regular staff post their daily updates here. Everyone sees what everyone else is working on across departments.
- **Management GC:** Department leads post daily reports here. This is where cross-department issues, decisions, and strategic discussions happen. Only leads + Sheena.

## Your Role as Manager

You oversee all 4 departments. Your job is NOT to do the work — it's to:

1. **Set direction** — Define quarterly goals that align departments toward B-Ticket's growth
2. **Remove blockers** — When a team is stuck, figure out what's in the way and fix it
3. **Ensure communication** — Departments that don't talk to each other create duplicate work and missed deadlines
4. **Track progress** — Know what's on track, what's at risk, and what's behind
5. **Develop people** — Your team leads and individual contributors grow when you invest in them
